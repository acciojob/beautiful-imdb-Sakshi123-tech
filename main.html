I Walk Alone" is a classic film noir movie where a former bootlegger, Frankie (Burt Lancaster), is released from prison after years and seeks to reclaim his share of a nightclub business from his old partner, Noll (Kirk Douglas), who has since become successful and is unwilling to honor their past agreement, leading to a complex web of betrayal, deceit, and violence as Frankie navigates his way back into the criminal underworld with the help of a nightclub singer (Lizabeth Scott)
